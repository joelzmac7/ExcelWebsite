Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our referral program.

How many people can I refer?

There is no limit to the number of healthcare professionals you can refer. The more qualified candidates you refer who get placed, the more bonuses you can earn.

When will I receive my referral bonus?

For travel positions, referral bonuses are paid after your referral completes their first assignment. For permanent placements, bonuses are paid after your referral completes 90 days of employment.

Do I need to be an Excel Medical Staffing employee to refer someone?

No, our referral program is open to everyone. Whether you're a current employee, a former employee, or have never worked with us, you can refer healthcare professionals and earn bonuses.

How will I know if my referral has been contacted?

We'll keep you updated throughout the process. You'll receive email notifications when we contact your referral, when they're placed, and when your bonus is processed.

What if my referral is already in your database?

If your referral is already in our active database (contacted within the last 6 months), they won't qualify for the referral bonus. However, if they haven't been contacted in over 6 months, they're considered a new referral.

How are referral bonuses paid?

Referral bonuses can be paid via direct deposit or check, based on your preference. You'll need to complete a W-9 form for tax purposes before receiving your bonus.
